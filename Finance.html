WHAT IS THE FIND TRUCK SERVICE CREDIT CARD?

Find Truck Service offers customers a credit card through Synchrony Bank with a revolving credit line of up to $10,000 to use again and again for services and parts at all Find Truck Service Authorized vendor locations.*

WHERE DO I CHECK THE STATUS OF MY ACCOUNT?

To check the status of your Find Truck Service credit card account, click on the Manage My Account button below to go to the Synchrony Bank website. After logging into your account, you will be able to:

• Make a payment
• View your balance
• Update your account information
• Schedule up to your next 12 payments

HOW DO I APPLY FOR A FIND TRUCK SERVICE CREDIT CARD?

You can apply for a Find Truck Service credit card online by clicking here and get a fast credit decision or you can visit any of our Authorized vendor locations to apply in person.

HOW DO I REQUEST A LIMIT INCREASED AFTER I APPLY?

You can simply call 866-657-0376 to request a credit limit increase on your online approval.

• Pay Online: Click here to manage your account and make payments online.
