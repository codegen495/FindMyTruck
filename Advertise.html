About Find Truck Service

Find Truck Service, Inc started in 2007 with the vision of providing the best national source of heavy duty service locations for truck drivers and trucking industry carriers. Our mission is to provide truck drivers and carriers with easy, reliable, and fast access to service locations across North America. With decades of combined experience in transportation and the heavy duty service industry we understand the needs. Our goal is to save drivers and carriers valuable time and money by providing them with easy access to thousands of qualified heavy duty service providers nationwide. We are dedicated 24 hours a day, all year round to provide the trucking industry with the best information, access and support.
